Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

You can book an appointment by calling us at (555) 123-BEAUTY, using our online booking system, or visiting us in person. We recommend booking in advance, especially for weekend appointments.

We require 24-hour notice for cancellations. Late cancellations or no-shows may be subject to a fee. We understand emergencies happen, so please call us to discuss your situation.

We welcome walk-ins when possible, but availability is limited. To guarantee your preferred time and stylist, we highly recommend booking an appointment in advance.

We accept cash, all major credit cards, debit cards, and digital payments including Apple Pay and Google Pay. Gift certificates are also available for purchase.
